About Us

When Naresh Jain was visiting Pune to work with a local company, he realized that there were many interesting companies in the area, but they all operated as islands. To break the ice and get the local techies to share their experience with each other, he decided to start a small meetup group called TechJam .

Naresh was really happy when he got support from IDeaS.com to host these meetups. Hopefully in future more companies will support this initiative and we'll spread out to other companies.

Please email Naresh, if:

  • you are interested in volunteering as a coordinator or
  • your company is interested in hosting a future meetup or
  • you are interested in presenting at our meetup
